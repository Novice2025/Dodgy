Literal vs. Real Meaning

**"Dodgy"** is an informal adjective with several meanings, all of which suggest something is not quite right. It's used to describe something or someone as unreliable, suspicious, of low quality, or potentially dangerous.

Think of it as the go-to word when you get a bad feeling about a person, a place, a product, or a situation.
